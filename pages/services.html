Unbalanced nutrition entails a lack of vitamins, trace elements, nutrients, obesity, which leads to health problems

30-60 minutes a day is physical activity, which reduces the risk of various diseases and helps to maintain optimal weight

Lack of sleep, especially during periods of active growth, negatively affects the immune system and overall health

There is an increase in the number of children with mental health disorders - depression, anxiety disorders and behavioral disorders. Monitor the emotional state of your child, maintain open communication with him, in difficult situations do not be afraid to consult a psychologist

Children who wash their hands with soap and water and use sanitizers are less likely to get infectious diseases. Keeping the house clean helps to reduce the spread of infection

Children are at risk of contracting various infectious diseases such as upper respiratory tract infections, influenza, viral hepatitis, etc. Careful adherence to the schedule i.e. immunization calendar ensures that your child develops timely immunity against infectious diseases. Check with your doctor to find out which immunizations are recommended for your child's age

Routine visits to the pediatrician can help detect and prevent illnesses and help prevent developmental problems

SOGAZ-Med Insurance Company recommends not to neglect routine medical examinations of children under 18 years of age.
