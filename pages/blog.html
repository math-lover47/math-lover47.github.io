News

New! Parking is free for client families starting November 1

At MedKz, our goal is to eliminate as many barriers to care as possible. From feedback we've received from families, parking fees and tickets are a barrier to accessing programs and services across all sites.

MedKz welcomes boost to funding for programs and services

MedKz is pleased to see the Government of Kazakhstan's commitment to funding for programs and services for children with special needs. The government has announced a $1.5 million investment in programs and services for children with special needs.

New and Enhanced Respite Services - Registration Now Open

MedKz is pleased to announce that registration is now open for new and enhanced respite services. These services are designed to provide families with a break from the demands of caring for a child with special needs.

Masks Still Required

MedKz continues to require masks for all clients, staff, and visitors. We are committed to keeping our clients and staff safe and healthy. Thank you for your cooperation.

What's New in Autism Services?

MedKz is pleased to announce that we are expanding our autism services. We are now offering a new program for children with autism. This program is designed to help children with autism develop the skills they need to succeed in school and in life.
