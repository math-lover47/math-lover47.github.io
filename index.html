About Us

Welcome to Kazakhstan's First Digital Rehabilitation Center for Children — a pioneering hub dedicated to empowering children and youth to realize their full potential. At our core, we believe every child deserves the opportunity to thrive and actively participate in their community. Through innovative clinical services, cutting-edge assistive technologies, comprehensive education, and groundbreaking research, we provide personalized support to children and their families. Our center is built on a foundation of equity, diversity, inclusion, and belonging. We are committed to authentically engaging with children, youth, and families from all walks of life, ensuring that everyone feels valued, heard, and supported on their journey. By leveraging the latest advancements in digital rehabilitation, we aim to break barriers, create opportunities, and help children and youth lead fulfilling, connected lives. Together, we are redefining what’s possible for the next generation. Join us as we build a future where every child can thrive, grow, and shine.

Technologies

Strolll

is cutting-edge augmented reality (AR) therapy system designed to enhance rehabilitation outcomes through engaging, client-centred activities.

Syrebo

is a high-tech solution for hand rehabilitation.

DST Triple Sense Parallel Bars

Developed by DPE Medical Ltd, the DST Triple Sense Parallel Bars combine three devices into one, offering an adjustable staircase, inclined walking surface, and horizontal bar.

Brain Recovery Zone

is an immersive virtual reality recovery platform, which offers a safe virtual space for people to practice the habits that aid recovery.

Sustainability in Rehabilitation

Luna EMG

Robotic device for upper and lower limb neurological and orthopedics rehabilitation. Experience revolution in neurorehabilitation and orthopedic therapy in your own practice.

Biomotum

The Biomotum SPARK is transforming mobility rehabilitation, helping those with walking impairments live without limits. Our on-demand robotic system is remarkably effective at assisting and restoring walking function when compared to conventional approaches.
